Welcome to Paintworks

At Paintworks, we are experts in high-quality residential spray finishing, bringing a superior level of craftsmanship to every project. Our professional painting services are designed to transform your home with a flawless, modern finish that traditional brush and roller methods simply can’t achieve.

A spray finish delivers an ultra-smooth, even coat with no visible brush strokes, ensuring a high-end look that enhances the beauty of any surface. To achieve this level of perfection, we prioritize meticulous preparation. Our process includes thorough mechanical sanding, precise filling of imperfections, and expert priming—ensuring a durable, long-lasting, and truly professional result.

With our commitment to quality and attention to detail, Paintworks guarantees a finish that not only looks stunning but also stands the test of time. Whether you're refreshing a single room or giving your entire home a new lease of life, we deliver outstanding results with efficiency and care.






With the Mirka Abranet sanding system, the dust is dramatically reduced with 99% of dust being caught by the dust extractor. All surfaces are machine sanded to give a smooth surface prior to paint application.

Airless spraying using high pressure to atomize the paint into fine particles that are apllied to the surface evenly. This creates a superior finish.

HVLP is a low pressure system which using a turbine to atomize the paint. This creates an ultra fine finish. It is especially suitable for furniture and other small items..
