Painting Preparation Process

Thorough Sanding

Achieving a flawless painted finish begins with meticulous surface preparation. We ensure that all flat surfaces are thoroughly machine-sanded to create an ultra-smooth foundation, allowing the paint to adhere perfectly and produce a professional, long-lasting result.

  • Initial Sanding (100-120 grit): Removes imperfections, old brush strokes, and rough patches.
  • Fine Sanding (180-240 grit): Refines the surface, ensuring a perfectly smooth texture.
  • Final Detailing: Hand-sanding of edges, corners, and intricate moldings for uniformity.

Cleaning

Once sanding is complete, we perform a deep cleaning process to remove dust, grease, or contaminants that could affect the quality of the finish.

  • Degreasing with Sugar Soap: Breaks down grime, fingerprints, and residue.
  • Dust Removal: Wiped down with a microfiber or tack cloth to eliminate particles.
  • Final Surface Check: Ensures a clean surface for paint application.

Protection

To maintain a clean workspace and safeguard surrounding areas, we take extensive precautions before painting begins.

  • Furniture & Flooring: Covered with dust sheets or plastic wrap; stairs secured with plastic protectors.
  • Electrical Fixtures & Hardware: Switch plates, sockets, are covered or removed to ensure a good finish.
  • Masking & Taping: High-quality tape is applied to achieve clean, crisp paint lines.

Workspace & Painting Process

Our painting process ensures a high-end, factory-quality finish using professional spray techniques.

  • Spray Application: Delivers a smooth, uniform coat without visible brush strokes.
  • Controlled Environment: Airflow and humidity are managed to prevent dust contamination.
  • Layering & Drying: Thin, even layers are applied with proper drying time for maximum durability.
